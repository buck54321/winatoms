The Contract

A challenge is a contract that exists on the Decred blockchain. The contract reads, in essence, "anyone posessing the game key and the solution can claim the funds". You fund the contract when you pay to the challenge's address.

Security

The important cryptographic operations that secure the contract are performed on your computer before submitting the challenge data. The game key, however, is generated and stored by Win Atoms!. The details of the contract are encoded in the game key, but not the solution. The redeemer must have both the solution and the game key, and Win Atoms! never collects a solution.

When the challenge is solved, the winner is given a copy of the game key and a pre-signed contract template from which a final contract can be assembled in your browser. To gain access to the game key, the winner does not submit the actual solution, but just cryptographic proof that they know the solution.

If the user chooses to use the instant redeem feature to relay their transaction, Win Atoms! does have access to the solution and the game key simultaneously, so could, in theory, steal the funds at that point. We won't.

Obviously, the challenge creator can always solve their own challenge.
